Field Guide

How to Play

01

Read the enemy

Watch the intent at the top of the screen. Close attacks can be countered, long attacks cannot, and status moves change the next turn.

02

Choose fast

Pick attack, defense, assist, or switch before the timer empties. Acting quickly builds extra momentum.

03

Defend with timing

When defending, press the shown key inside the timing window. Counters hit back only against close attacks.

04

Use the team

Switch characters to match the fight. Inactive allies recover slowly, and assists can stabilize a dangerous turn.

05

Adapt to the boss

The final boss can force switches and lock the arena. Keep the whole team alive and respond to each phase.

06

Protect your grade

Your score rewards team HP, max combo, and counters. Penalties, defeats, and timeouts lower the final grade.

Battle
A full fight that ends when the enemy is defeated or all player characters fall.
Turn
One action taken by either the player or the enemy.
Round
A pair of turns: the player acts, then the enemy resolves its action.
Intent
The enemy's telegraphed next action, shown before you choose your move.
Combo
Your momentum multiplier. Good play raises it, mistakes lower it, and damage scales with it.
Counter
A risky timed defense that only works against close attacks and can hit the enemy back.
Dodge
A timed defense that avoids direct attack damage when successful.
Block
The safest defense. It reduces damage and gives a small combo gain.
Assist
A support action from an inactive character that can heal, weaken enemies, or create openings.
Switch
Change the active character. Each battle allows one normal manual switch.
Meltdown
A dangerous state triggered when Tiger reaches 0 HP. Girl becomes inactive and other characters fight under unstable pressure.
Grade
Your battle rating based on HP, combo, counters, fast actions, and penalties.
